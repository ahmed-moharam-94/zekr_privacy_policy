# Zekr Privacy Policy
The Zekr application is a global platform for teaching and learning the Holy Quran, enabling users to read the Quran individually, communicate with expert teachers as independent contractors, and conduct live audio sessions for recitation correction, memorization, or Tajweed. By creating an account, users enter into a binding agreement governed by the laws of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, acknowledging that Zekr acts strictly as a technical intermediary and is not responsible for direct interactions with teachers. The platform includes a motivational points program where users can earn up to ten promotional, non-cash points daily for reading, which may be exchanged for rewards from partners, though Zekr reserves the absolute right to modify or terminate this program without liability. Users must be at least 18 years old or use the app under parental supervision, and they are prohibited from fraudulent activities, such as creating multiple accounts or using automated programs. Zekr maintains a privacy policy where sessions may be recorded for quality and dispute resolution, and users retain rights to access, correct, or delete their personal data. Fees for sessions and subscriptions are paid in advance and are generally non-refundable, with app store policies applying to external purchases. Zekr retains all intellectual property rights and may amend the agreement at any time, with continued use of the application serving as implicit consent to these changes.
